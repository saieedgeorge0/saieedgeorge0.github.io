Schedule

The day will begin with a church parade at 10:00 am, and will conclude around 8:00 pm. It will take place at the park on the corner of 11th Street and Merchant Avenue (in Ambridge), one block from the church. Many of the different churches will have booths which sell ethnic food and crafts, put on performances, contain free petting zoos and put on other activities for kids. Our church will have a table selling food and religious/cultural items. We should have fun and decorate our booth and share our wonderful culture with the community! We need help to prepare the food ahead of time and to work the booth during the festival.

Sign-Up to Bring Food

Ideas for what to bring: stuffed grape leaves, falafel sandwiches, grilled chicken and kofta sandwiches, spinach and cheese pies, baklava, cookies, etc.


Contact

Contact Mary-Ann Wahba with any questions - if you'd like to participate in the church parade, let her know, as you will need to be there earlier than most (around 9:15 am).
