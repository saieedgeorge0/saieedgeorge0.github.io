Run for Cover is the University of Chicago's award-winning all-male a cappella group. Founded in 2007 by a group of 5 students, it has grown to become one of the prominent all-male groups in the Midwest. The group performs at events both within and outside of the university, and competed in the International Championship of Collegiate A Cappella in 2014, advancing to the Semifinal round. In the past two years, Run for Cover has released its debut album and two singles, which you can find on iTunes and Spotify. This year, the group is currently in the process of recording and releasing its second album. We always welcome new gigs and would love to sing for you - visit our contact us page in order to get in touch!
